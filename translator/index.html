# State Shorthand A tiny, symbol-based check-in system: **State + Direction + Cause**. It’s designed for real life: work, family, teams, journaling, ADHD brains, introverts, loud extroverts—anyone who wants to communicate *how they are* without writing a paragraph. ## The Format **[STATE][DIRECTION] (CAUSE)** - **STATE** = how you feel right now - **DIRECTION** = where it’s going - **CAUSE** (optional) = what’s driving it Example: - **△→ (W)** = restless/irritated, staying that way, because of work --- ## State Symbols Pick one: - **○** = steady / okay / regulated - **△** = restless / irritated / unsettled - **✕** = overloaded / overwhelmed / too much - **↓** = drained / tired / low battery - **↑** = energized / hyped / ready to move > Tip: You can customize these meanings slightly for your household or team. The goal is “fast and true,” not “perfect.” --- ## Direction Arrows Pick one: - **↑** = improving - **→** = staying the same - **↓** = getting worse Examples: - **✕↑** = overwhelmed, but improving - **↓→** = tired, staying tired - **○↓** = okay, but slipping --- ## Cause Tags (Optional) Add 1 letter (or 2 if needed): - **W** = work / school - **B** = body (sleep, hunger, illness, hormones) - **M** = mind (overthinking, anxiety, focus, stress) - **S** = social (people, conflict, crowds) - **H** = home / family logistics - **F** = faith / spiritual (peace, dryness, conviction, etc.) Examples: - **✕→ (W+M)** = overwhelmed and stuck, work + mind - **↓↑ (B)** = tired but recovering, body-related - **○→ (F)** = steady and stable, faith-related --- ## Why This Works - **Privacy with signal** (no forced explanations) - **Low-friction honesty** - **Pattern spotting over time** (you’ll notice what consistently causes △→ or ✕↓) - **Family-friendly** (kids can use symbols only; teens/adults can add cause tags) --- ## Quick Examples - **△→ (W)** “I’m irritated and it’s not changing. Work.” - **✕↑ (M)** “I was overloaded, but I’m improving. Mind stress.” - **↓→ (B)** “I’m tired and staying tired. Body.” - **↑→** “I’m energized and stable.” - **○↓ (S)** “I’m okay but slipping because of social stuff.” --- ## License MIT — steal it, remix it, teach it, rename it, do whatever. State Shorthand
